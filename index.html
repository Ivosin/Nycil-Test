Welcome to Nycil Limited

...where we have a guaranteed quality rating of 99.8% over the years.

NYCIL Limited was incorporated in 1978 operating from Otta, Ogun State under the Name Nigerian Synthetic Chemical Industry Limited. It started off as a four-type Resins manufacturer but has progressed to include positioning for profitable growth in Performance Technologies with over 150 types of Resins and Chemicals in its product range to Allied Industries and Oil and Chemicals. This has been made possible because of her Excellent Production and Research & Development team as well as the visionary outlook of her Management team and a dedicated work force.

Our high standard in production is committed to ensure maximum consumers satisfaction. This is propelled by standard Production achieved through Excellent Research and Development. We Take Pride in Servicing Nigerian Industries: PAINTS, TEXTILE, ADHESIVE, PACKAGING, PVA, STATIONERY, etc.
